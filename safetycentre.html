Common Scams in Kenya Right Now

Scammers use the same tricks over and over. If you see these, run:

1. "Pay KES 500 for registration"

Real jobs never ask YOU to pay. If they want money before giving you work, block and report. Legit clients pay you, not the other way.

2. "We found your CV online, job is ready!"

They message out of nowhere on WhatsApp or Telegram. No interview, no details, just "send ID + M-Pesa". It’s fake. Real companies email from official domains.

3. "Earn 10K per day typing"

If the pay sounds crazy for the work, it's a lie. Data entry jobs pay KES 500-2000 per day max. Don’t fall for it.

Your Safety Checklist

Before you start any gig, ask yourself:

  1. Do I know the company name and location?
  2. Is the payment clear before I start?
  3. Have I told a friend where I'm going if it's in-person?
  4. Am I being asked to send money first?

If you answered "yes" to #4, stop immediately.

Got Scammed? Don't Be Ashamed

It happens to smart people too. Here's what to do:

Step 1: Screenshot everything - chats, numbers, M-Pesa messages

Step 2: Report to Safaricom: Forward scam SMS to 333

Step 3: Tell your friends so they don't fall for the same trick
